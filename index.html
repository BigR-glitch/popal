The photographer

Junaid
Popal

Originally from Afghanistan, now rooted in London — Junaid Popal has spent five years building a visual language that bridges cultures, captures honesty, and finds beauty in the unremarkable.

Based in South Kensington, he works across lifestyle, portrait, and film — always with the same philosophy: don't arrange the world, observe it.

His clients range from personal brand builders to event organisers across London. He is available for commissions across the UK and internationally.
